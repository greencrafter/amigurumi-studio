About Us

Amigurumi Studio was born from a deep passion for penguins and the art of amigurumi crochet. What started as a simple hobby creating penguin-inspired toys quickly grew into a beloved studio of handcrafted characters.

Every stitch is a reflection of our love for this whimsical craft. We take pride in designing joyful creatures that bring smiles to kids and adults alike. Thank you for being part of our journey.
